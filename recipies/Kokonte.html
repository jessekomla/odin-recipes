Kokonte

Kokonte is also known as Black sanyaa or Dzi Dzi

This is a typical food of the great good and healthy Ghanaian

Ingredients

  • Black sanyaa
  • Ekpili mawc
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Mix the Ekpili mawc
  2. Stir and stir until it thinkens
  3. Grind the tomatos and onions and pepper
  4. There you have it
